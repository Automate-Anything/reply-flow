Bottom Bar Advantages

  • One-tap access to key sections
  • Thumb-friendly (bottom of screen)
  • Matches WhatsApp/Slack patterns users know
  • Shows unread badges easily

Hamburger Advantages

  • More vertical screen space
  • Can show all nav items at once
  • Already implemented in your codebase
  • Less work to keep current approach
